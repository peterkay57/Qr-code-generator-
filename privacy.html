Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Ad Astra is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe that your digital tools should help you, not track you. This policy explains how we handle—or more accurately, don't handle—your personal information.

2. No Data Collection

We collect zero personal information. We do not ask for your name, your email, or your phone number. We do not log your IP address. We don't even know who you are when you use the app.

3. Local Storage Only

Any data you save within the app—such as your 2FA accounts, your master password, or your secure vault entries—is stored only on your local device. This data is encrypted and never leaves your browser. We never transmit your secrets to any external server.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising SDKs, or any other third-party tracking services. Your usage is completely anonymous. QR code generation and scanning happen directly in your browser without communicating with our servers.

5. No Cookies

Ad Astra does not use tracking cookies. We may use standard browser storage (like LocalStorage or IndexedDB) purely to keep the app working and to store your encrypted vault locally.

6. Camera Permissions

When you use the QR Scanner, the app will ask for camera permission. This is used solely to detect QR codes. The video stream is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, saved, or uploaded anywhere.

7. Internet Connection

As a web-based application, Ad Astra requires an internet connection to initially load. Once loaded, however, your data management and QR generation occur locally. No data is sent to our servers during your session.

8. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, Ad Astra is safe for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect information from anyone.
